John Mayer Trio

Continuum
Back in September we reported that John Mayer had revealed in a tweet upcoming plans to go “back in the studio with the Continuum crew in January.” The Continuum crew would be drummer Steve Jordan and bassist Pino Palladino who make up the John Mayer Trio that recorded and released the Trio’s live album Try! in 2005.
