Dreamtown

Background

Dreamtown is a Stockholm based band playing atmospheric indierock with elements of folk music. The band released their debut EP in March, including songs built around the vocal harmonies of the two brother lead singers, well-crafted guitar lines, piano, bass and drums.

Band members

Elias Lundström: Vocals, Guitar

Oscar Karlsson: Guitar
Gustaf Lundström: Guitar
Fredrik Ek: Bass

Samuel Teleki: Drums
Fredrik Hörnesten: Piano
